About the Show

Come experience a cutting-edge musically synchronized Christmas light show this season! This six-minute show features over 2400 color-changing lights synchronized to Christmas pop, hip-hop, and movie sound tracks with funny jokes and puns mixed in. Simply park in your assigned spot (we'll tell you when you get there), tune your vehicle's radio to the posted FM station, and enjoy the show!

FAQ

How many lights are there?

There are about 10,000 individually color-changing LED lights covering over 200 feet of the building all moving in sync to music!

How long does it take to set up?

Planning for next year's show starts the previous December. Lights are ordered in January, props are built through the summer, and programming and installation happen in the fall. It is a never-ending process!

The electric bill must be crazy!

Surprisingly, the show uses much less electricity than you'd think! The whole show runs off the same amount of power as a hairdryer!

I'm a geek - how does it all work?

The show is programmed using a software called xLights and is played through Falcon Player (FPP) to a network of pixel controllers distrubuted throughout the show.
